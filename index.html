What is Ulead?

ULead is Unschool’s flagship internship program for students to upskill themselves while reinventing learning and transforming lives with us. Embark on a fulfilling journey to experience, create, nurture and carry out multiple aspects of what makes our organization click, right from sales, to marketing in various segments.

Proudly funded by Y Combinator

Ranked 3rd on LinkedIn's Top Startups 2020

Levels of Ulead Program

As you move up the ladder, you’ll have your own team. Leadership is the most sought-after skill by all companies, and Ulead is the place to be if you see yourself as a corporate leader in the future.

Youth Influencer

This will be Level 1 of this internship program. Youth Influencers works on marketing Unschool Courses and gaining traction online, building operations and getting matches, and advertising on different social media platforms. Get Promoted to the next level on successful completion of the given tasks.

Youth Leader

This will be Level 2 of this internship program. Youth Leaders lead the revolution by building and mentoring a team and and ace at leadership skills and operations. They represent Unschool at events and build marketing and operations strategies.

Roles and Responsibilities

Your role is to take the concept of Unschooling far & wide and empower students to become industry-ready through courses and internships.

01

SALES AND BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

Branding and promotion of Unschool courses are the primary tasks of your internship. Market Unschool’s courses in your college, neighborhood, and city and get exciting rewards on each enrollment.

02

EXPAND TO DIFFERENT COLLEGES

The next step becomes taking over the city, in true Leader style. Expand your reach to more than just a few colleges. Initiate ULead in different colleges.

03

MARKETING AND USAGE OF SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS

Marketing through various social media channels is very important to learn and master skills that can help towards the organic lead generation

04

EVENTS, WORKSHOPS, AND PARTNERSHIPS

Be the representative of Unschool in your college and build student clubs to Approach student bodies, Class Representatives to enhance our branding

Application Criteria and Process

Actively enrolled in a school or college/ university, in any course or year.

Strong leadership and interpersonal skills.

Preferred experience in college fests, societies, or clubs.

Most importantly, the drive to Upskill.

We are looking for spirited individuals who are looking to challenge the way the system works and If you believe that exceptional and well-rounded education is the way to change the world, we are on the same page.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much stipend will I get?

    • Earn while learning. Ulead is a performance-based incentive program, which means your stipend depends on your performance. There is no limit to what you can earn from the Ulead internship program. Well-performing interns will also get a Pre-placement offer.

  • Should we pay for this internship?

    • No, you don't have to pay any amount for the Ulead internship program. Just fill out the registration form and our executive team will contact you. You just have to clear the interview.

  • Who can apply?

    • Students in higher education, under graduation and post-graduation and having Strong leadership and interpersonal skills, can apply. Preferred experience in college fests, societies, or clubs.

  • How long is this internship?

    • The tenure of this internship will be one month. If you perform well during the internship you will get an opportunity to extend your tenure.

  • What are the working hours?

    • The working hours are flexible. All of your work during the internship will be after college hours and it will not coincide with your studies, exams, or other college activities.

  • How much stipend will I get ?

    • In this internship programme we are offering Performance-Based incentives and benefits. Well-performing interns will also get promotions.

  • What is the application process?

    • Interested students can fill the application form on the Ulead website with the required credentials.

  • What happens after we apply?

    • Interested College students can fill the application form on the Ulead website with the required credentials.
